Process

How Private Lending Works

A straightforward process from initial conversation to return of your capital plus interest.

1

Initial Conversation

We discuss your investment goals, timeline, and comfort level. We'll share our approach, our track record, and answer every question you have.

2

Deal Presentation

When we have a deal that fits, we present the full picture — purchase price, renovation budget, comparable sales, projected timeline, and your expected return.

3

Fund & Secure

Your investment is secured by the property through proper legal documentation. You're protected by the equity in the deal and adequate insurance coverage.

4

Renovate & Sell

We execute the renovation, list the property, and sell it. Throughout the process, you receive regular updates on progress and timeline.

5

Get Paid

When the property sells, you receive your principal plus agreed-upon interest. Typical hold times are 6-12 months.

Risk Management

How We Protect Your Investment

Conservative Underwriting

We only pursue deals with significant built-in equity. Our purchase price plus renovation costs always stays well below the after-repair value, creating a cushion that protects your capital.

Experienced Execution

With over $20 million in projects, we've built reliable systems and contractor relationships. We know what renovations cost, how long they take, and what buyers want. No learning on your dime.

Multiple Exit Strategies

Every deal we enter has at least two exit paths — typically retail sale and rental refinance. If the market shifts, we have options. Your capital is never dependent on a single outcome.

Regular Communication

You're never in the dark. We provide regular updates with photos, financials, and timeline projections throughout every project. Ask us anything, anytime.
