About Fenner Flips

Meet the Founders

Charlie and Erin Fenner

Charlie and Erin are a Nashville-based husband-and-wife team with a deep connection to the local community and a shared passion for real estate. Charlie brings years of hands-on experience in buying, renovating, and selling homes across the Nashville area, with a reputation for identifying opportunities and delivering high-quality results.

Erin, a Nashville native, offers invaluable local insight and a lifelong understanding of the neighborhoods, growth, and character that make the city unique. Together, they combine experience, local knowledge, and a personal approach to help clients and partners navigate real estate with confidence.

At Fenner Flips, we buy houses in any condition, renovate them to a high standard, and put beautiful homes back on the market. Every project creates value — for the seller, for the community, and for our investment partners.

We've completed over $20 million in projects and we're always looking for our next opportunity. If you have a house to sell or capital to invest, we'd love to talk!
