FAQ

Common Questions

How do you determine your offer price?
We look at recent comparable sales in your area, the condition of your property, and the cost of needed repairs. Our goal is to make a fair offer that works for both of us. We're transparent about how we arrive at our numbers.
Do I need to make any repairs before selling?
Absolutely not. We buy houses in any condition — from move-in ready to major fixer-uppers. Don't spend a dime on repairs. That's our job.
Are there any fees or commissions?
None. We don't charge any fees, commissions, or closing costs. The offer we make is the amount you receive at closing.
How fast can you close?
We can close in as few as 14 days. Most sales close in 14-21 days. But if you need more time, we'll work around your timeline — you pick the date.
What if my house is in foreclosure?
We work with homeowners in foreclosure regularly. Depending on your timeline, we may be able to close before the foreclosure sale and help you avoid the impact on your credit. Contact us as soon as possible so we can explore your options.
Is there any obligation if I request an offer?
No obligation whatsoever. Our offer is 100% free. If it doesn't work for you, no hard feelings. We want you to feel confident in your decision.
