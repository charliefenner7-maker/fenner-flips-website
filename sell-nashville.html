Nashville FAQ

Common Questions

Do you buy houses in any Nashville neighborhood?

Yes. We buy throughout Davidson County — East Nashville, Donelson, Antioch, Bordeaux, Inglewood, Hermitage, Old Hickory, Bellevue and everywhere in between. If your house is in or around Nashville, we want to make you an offer.

Can you close before a foreclosure sale in Nashville?

Often, yes. Davidson County foreclosures move on a set timeline, and if you reach out early enough we can frequently close before the sale date and help you avoid the credit damage. Call us as soon as possible so we can look at your options.

My Nashville house needs a lot of work — will you still buy it?

Absolutely. A huge share of Nashville's housing stock is older and needs work, and that is exactly what we do. Foundation, roof, full gut — we buy as-is and you spend nothing on repairs.
